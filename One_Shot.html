Review

Today I visited One Shot Cafe this coffee shop is located in the Northern Liberties part of Philadelphia and is one of my favorite coffee shops of all time. Not only do they serve coffee but they have an extensive selection of pastries (some gluten free), teas, and some breakfast and lunch food options. Not only are the drinks delicious but the ambiance and atmosphere is so adorable you want to stay there forever.

For coffee options they have delicious brewed coffee but one of the best things to try when visiting are their gourmet latte’s., one of the most exciting ones I tried was their marshmallow praline sweet potato latte. Now I believe this drink was seasonally exclusively in the fall which is perfect for you to go grab one now.

Two of the down falls of this coffee shop are parking and price. If you are planning on driving there finding street parking is tricky so be prepared to either drive around for a while or just bite the bullet and park a distance away. The prices are pretty steep but I have always found the price well worth the vibe and quality of the drinks I have ever purchased there. Another note is they do not take cards for purchases under ten dollars so be sure to have some cash on you.

Bring a friend, a good book, or some homework and go enjoy the beauty of One Shot Cafe, It won’t disappoint!!
