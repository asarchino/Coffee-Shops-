Review

OCF Coffee is one of the most relaxing coffee shops out there, not only is there coffee delicious but they have delicious salads, sandwiches, and pastries. Another plus is the abundance of gluten free items they offer and how knowledgeable they are about what is gluten free.

The Location of OCF is another plus as it is located across from eastern state penitentiary which is a beautiful building the shop looks out over. The atmosphere inside OCF is friendly and leaves you feeling comfortable. The seating inside is abundant which I appreciate because some coffee shops have very little seating.

A must when going to OCF is trying their iced teas, they already have some brewed but they will also fresh brew and ice any tea you want. This is one of the best parts about this place, but also the staff is lovely. They are so friendly and helpful and are always willing to answer any of my questions about drinks or food. Their coffee is just delicious as well, try a dirty chai or a yummy vanilla latte and it wonâ€™t disappoint.

Parking is abundant around the Fairmount location, which is helpful when traveling to OCF. Enjoy this fabulous location and delicious coffee!!
