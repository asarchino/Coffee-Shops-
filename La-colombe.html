Review

La Colombe is a famous coffee company within the united states. Their flagship location is huge and is located in a beautiful rustic building in Fishtown Philadelphia. From the moment you lay eyes the building you will automatically fall in love with it. Inside the store there is an abundance of seating, to relax and chat or crack open a good book. The one issue I have with this coffee shop is that they do not have wifi so this is a hard place to go if you are looking to do online work.

One the other hand their coffee drinks, food and pastries are delicious. The draft latte is out of this world, seriously it is one of my all time favorite things in the world. It is a must! One of the best things about this location is the numerous amount of food options they offer. From soup to sandwiches to grits and eggs the food will not disappoint.

Parking is a tab bit difficult but a little perseverance will get you a long way in regards to parking.
